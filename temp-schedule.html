History of AI:   

1940s-50s – Dartmouth workshop; very “rule-based” approaches  

Alan Turing (1950) – Proposed the idea of machine intelligence in Computing Machinery and Intelligence and introduced the Turing Test  

Early Neural Networks (1943-1958) – Warren McCulloch & Walter Pitts created a simplified artificial neuron model, forming the foundation for later deep learning. Discovery of neurons  

1970s: AI winter – data-driven ML ideas exist, but too computationally expensive  

1990s-2000s? Rise of machine learning, breakthroughs in games (IBM Deep Blue etc)  

2000s+ explosion of data, cheap storage + compute, driving modern deep learning/data-driven approaches  

AI Assistants (2010s) – Siri, Google Assistant, and Alexa showcased natural language processing (NLP) advancements.  

Visual Map/defining terminology: Machine Learning, Supervised Learning, Unsupervised Learning
