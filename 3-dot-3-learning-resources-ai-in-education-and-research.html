What uses of AI are acceptable at university?

The use of Artificial Intelligence at university is a complex question. Where and when it is appropriate can often be difficult to gauge. In the first instance, always check the rules for the specific task. All assignments should tell you how you are allowed to use AI.

That said, not all AI usage is directly in service of an assignment. The following quiz is designed to give general guidance. Hopefully, it will improve your instincts for judging acceptable AI use.
