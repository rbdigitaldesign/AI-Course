This topic explores how AI is transforming the way we learn and conduct research. Whether you're just starting university or deep into your academic journey, AI tools are increasingly part of the educational landscape. But with these new capabilities come important questions: What’s allowed? What’s ethical? And how can you use AI to support, not replace, your learning?

We’ll look at how AI can assist with learning, research design, academic writing, and even presentations. You’ll also learn how to use AI responsibly, understand academic integrity, and reflect on your own use of these tools.

By the end of this topic, you will know:

  • Identify acceptable and unacceptable uses of AI in education and research contexts.
  • Use AI tools to support learning and research, including summarisation, prompting, and research design.
  • Reflect on the role of AI in their own education and academic development, including how it aligns with ethical guidelines.
  • Communicate and attribute AI use appropriately in academic work.
