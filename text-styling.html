Intro text

This text would be used at the start of a module/week/important section - kind of like a byline. Draw the eye, orient the user, give them some direction before jumping into the content.

Welcome to the first module: Curiosity.

The aim of this course is to develop your talent for framing and re-framing problems, exploring and re-imagining new possibilities, and developing solutions as opportunities. This will involve you working with one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), and exploring why the goal is necessary, and how we might achieve it.

You will adopt techniques used by entrepreneurs and designers to ignite imagination, pursue possibilities, and put you into the driver’s seat to achieve change.

You are in control of this journey, which could see you engaging through commercial, social or community new venture initiatives; research and development programs; and government or non-government enterprise.
