How to use AI at work

AI has already infiltrated many parts of our daily lives. This is no different in the work place. The main difference is, that workplaces often want to use AI to do new things (rather than just buy an existing product). The difference between a successful AI project and an unsuccessful one comes down to having a clear goal, the right resources and the right technical skills available. Another way of thinking about it, is asking the right questions at the right time.

Another challenge in building AI systems is communication. Building AI systems is a very technically complex challenge. Not only are there the engineers involved in building the AI system itself, but there is often other infrastructure (devices, networks etc.). This is before you even consider what the AI is actually for (could be medical, legal, agriculture etc.), who uses it (customers) and who needs to worry about running the project (project managers, finance, etc.). Communication is key. Being able to talk about AI systems at different levels of complexity and detail is key to a successful project.
