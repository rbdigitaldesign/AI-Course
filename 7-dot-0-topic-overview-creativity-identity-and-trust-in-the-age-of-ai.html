Our focus on this topic is to consider how AI impacts creativity. How can you trust what you see?

We explore some of the most complex and contested impacts of AI, those that affect culture, truth, and security. AI is not just a technical tool; it’s a cultural force. It shapes how we create, communicate, and understand the world around us. But with this power comes risk: misinformation, deepfakes, cultural erasure, and threats to digital and physical security.

We’ll look at how AI is used in creative industries, how it can both preserve and distort cultural knowledge, and how synthetic media challenges our ability to trust what we see and hear. You’ll engage with real and AI-generated artefacts, hear from experts, and reflect on the ethical dilemmas these technologies present.
