Tutor Instructions

Overview

This week's class will focus on groups establishing the topic and structure of their ePortfolio and Poster. This long-term work is to be done in groups, so it is important to have the groups established by now. 

Write down for future reference the student names and IDs of each group. Also write down which students are absent today (and the groups from which they are absent).

 

Task #1: Group Project Ideas

Explain to students that today they will start to devise ideas for their project groups. This means that they need to negotiate with each other to decide what their project will consist of. This could be:

  • Produce a creative work
  • Solve a problem
  • Test the feasibility of solving a problem
  • Investigate something
  • OR propose reform / a change 

Students can refer to the existing long list of project examples to inspire their thinking.

Students must decide on a project identity and title within this lesson, so circulate the room and guide them in their conversations.
