The Ready for Teaching Checklist is a collation of items designed to ensure that all Adelaide University staff are prepared for teaching in 2026. 

Follow the steps below to complete the checklist:

  1. Work through the checklist by adding Yes, No or N/A in the 'Completed' column next to each item.  
  2. Record any additional information about any in-progress or not applicable items in the 'Notes' area at the end of the page.
  3. Then follow up and complete any items that have been identified as in progress.

Ready for Teaching Checklist 

Please work through the checklist below and write Yes, No or N/A in the 'Completed' column next to each item.  
Yes = Completed, No = In Progress, N/A = Not Applicable
