Task #3: Reflection on Art (15 mins)

As a group, share your created artworks. Discuss your process, successes and failures.

As part of your discussion, consider the following:

  • What was your contribution to the 'art'.
  • How much do you feel like you 'own' in the process.
  • Do people like the art?
  • Is it art?

Task #4: Disruptive Technologies (20 mins)

There are numerous stakeholders when it comes to AI art. These include:

  • AI creators - people who want to use AI to create art
  • Traditional Artists - people's whose work may feed into generative AI tools
  • AI companies - people who build these AI tools
  • Consumers - people who benefit from the art

As a group, assign each person to one of these roles and spend 5 minutes to jot down some ideas about how AI art would impact your life (both positively and negatively) if you were in that role. After you have created your notes, have a discussion where each person takes on a different perspective. Consider the following questions:

  • Who benefits the most?
  • Who 'owns' the art.
  • Is anyone being 'cheated'?
  • How could people be compensated for the value they add?

Task #5: Identity and Security Reflection (15 mins)

It is now possible to create a deep fake video from a single image. This means that one could take a photo a person posted on social media (or elsewhere) and create a video of that person saying almost anything. With a bit of recorded audio, you could even copy their voice. By yourself, write down your answers to the following questions. Be prepared to discuss them with your group.

  • What security measures will you take to protect your identity in an AI-saturated world?
  • How does this make you feel?
  • How should companies be securing AI Systems
  • How do you feel about the risks of synthetic media, misinformation, and the need for robust governance?

Once everyone has had a chance to express their individual thoughts, discuss these questions as a table. There is no right answer here. Instead, try to understand other people's perspectives.
