1. The tutor will support a curated discussion (round tables) where students are given structured questions/problems posed as a real-world AI task.

 

Every 10 minutes new information / considerations are discussed as a whole group .. and then the tables go back to continuing their discussions. 

 

Currated discussion points could include: 

Data: consider is this image only … is it multimodal taking into account weather conditions and recent sightings; 

How will image data be collected in real time (is it processed in real time or time delay?) 

How will the information be made available to the authorities / general public 

Legal considerations: What is an acceptable level of accuracy; what happens if the system makes a mistake? 

Privacy concerns (ie capturing images of individuals / children) Etc as per items discussed in previous classes 

 

 

2. In groups develop a handout/take away of questions when designing an AI solution
