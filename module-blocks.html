Module overview blocks

Individually, these are restyled callouts, use a 3-column layout with simple AU-branded icons, similar to design patterns found online: https://adelaideuni.edu.au/study/how-to-apply/ 

Grey/default

Topic 1
Navigating the course foundations

We’ll explore the concept of Curiosity, Sustainable Development Goals, and discover the techniques of enquiry for design.

Topic 2
Environment, social, and economic sustainability

We’ll investigate what Responsible Innovation means. Then we’ll discover different stakeholder viewpoints on sustainability, and practice interviewing with empathy and using mind mapping.

Topic 3
Designing and delivering value

We’ll examine why entrepreneurial thinking is useful and how framing problems and perspectives helps to identify innovative solutions. Finally, we reflect on behavioural strengths that contribute to teams striving for innovation and achieving higher performances.
