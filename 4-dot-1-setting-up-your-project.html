The Project details and requirements 

  • Provide structure and guidance for what it looks like. Create a set of possible options. 
  • Template out some eportfolio samples with headers/pages aligned with assessment pieces. 
  • Pitch 100 problems, choose your own adventure, not about solving but about the journey of using the AI knowledge (knowing what AI is, how it can be used, what is responsible/reasonable use in different disciplines). 
  • Your project could be creative (ie finding a solution to a problem), investigative (using AI to help find knowledge to assist in solving a problem) or explorative (thinking about what AI could do to assist with this problem) or anything else your tutor feels is appropriate (just ask)
  • Project example: 
    • Make board games more interesting 
    • How to make travel safer 
    • Teams enterprise licence for the groups to use. Instructions on how to use.
