This week, we will dip our toes into how AI actually works: how it is built, what it does and how that impacts how we can use it. We'll look at a brief history of AI, as well as discussing data and how it is used to feed the AI behemoths. Finally, we will also start experimenting with AIs and working out how to get the best out of them.
