  6. Commons Workshop:
    Schedule: AI and the Future [0-40 discussion in groups, then bring back to the class for the last 20]

    - What is the most impressive thing you’ve seen AI do recently—and why did it stand out?
    - Why do humans consistently get predictions about technology wrong?
    - If someone in 1995 tried to predict today, what would they have missed, and are we making the same mistake now?
    - Which parts of your future job do you think AI will do better than you?
    - Which parts will remain distinctly human, and for how long?
    - Could AI increase inequality? How?
    - Should AI be allowed to make decisions that affect human lives (e.g., hiring, policing, healthcare)?
    - If an AI makes a bad decision, who will be responsible?
    - If AI can think, write, and create—what makes humans unique?
    - Which science fiction ideas about AI feel closest to reality today?
