During the workshop

Task 1: Introductions
  10 min

 Introduce yourself to your partner. Use pen and paper first to perform active listening [placeholder to describe what that is]. You will need your notes

Task 2: Playing with AI prompts
  30 min

Choose an AI tool (generative text [placeholder examples]) and use it to write you an introduction for your neighbour (hopefully you took good notes). Play with different prompt styles – formal vs casual introduction, use places where the responses from LLM require more detail to ask further questions of your partner and update the prompts. Explore different tones – nonchalant, dramatic movie trailer, Dr Seuss-style, ... Example prompts:

  • Try it in a nonchalant tone, as if I’m not interested in doing this introduction.
  • Introduce my partner as if they are a legendary figure in an epic fantasy novel.
  • Write a dramatic movie trailer voiceover introducing my partner as if they are the hero in an action blockbuster.
  • Write my partner's introduction as a Shakespearean monologue filled with unnecessary drama.

Task 3: Group Discussion
#1: AI tips and tricks
  15 min

 What did you learn? What type of prompts did you use to improve accuracy?

Task 4: Group Discussion
#2: AI in your life
  30 min

Artificial intelligence is becoming more and more present in our daily lives. How do you use AI in your daily life? Consider:

  • Active (AI tools) vs passive use
  • How often do you use AI and when?
  • What would happen if these tools became unavailable? How inconvenient does that make your life?
