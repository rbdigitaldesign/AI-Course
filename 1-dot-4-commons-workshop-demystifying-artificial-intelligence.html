During the workshop

Task 0: Tutor Introductions

  5 min

 Your tutor will introduce themselves and some of the basic logistics of the Commons Workshop and the course more generally.

Task 1: Introductions
  10 min

 Introduce yourself to your partner. Use pen and paper first. Perform active listening and take notes of essential information spoken by your learning partner.

Task 2: Playing with AI prompts
  30 min

Choose an AI tool (Big List of Tools) and use it to write you an introduction for your neighbour (hopefully you took good notes). Play with different prompt styles – formal vs casual introduction, use places where the responses from LLM require more detail to ask further questions of your partner and update the prompts. Explore different tones – nonchalant, dramatic movie trailer, Dr Seuss-style, ... Example prompts:

  • Try it in a nonchalant tone, as if I’m not interested in doing this introduction.
  • Introduce my partner as if they are a legendary figure in an epic fantasy novel.
  • Write a dramatic movie trailer voiceover introducing my partner as if they are the hero in an action blockbuster.
  • Write my partner's introduction as a Shakespearean monologue filled with unnecessary drama.

Task 3: Group Discussion
#1: AI tips and tricks
  15 min

What did you learn? What type of prompts did you use to improve accuracy?

Each table could demo their best output?

Task 4: Story Creation
  10 min

At your tables, you need to create a story together... one word at a time. You will do this by saying one word, with the next word spoken by the next person at the table. Continue going around the table until you are stopped (consider recording your story using AI speech-to-text).

Once / upon / a / time / there / ... 

Task 5: Group Discussion
#2: AI in your life
  30 min

Artificial intelligence is becoming more and more present in our daily lives. How do you use AI in your daily life? Consider:

  • Active (AI tools) vs passive use
  • How often do you use AI and when?
  • What would happen if these tools became unavailable? How inconvenient does that make your life?
  • Use Miro to focus discussion. Your tutor will host the main board and you will access via this link/QR code.  [link. Set up in advance for all tutors] (Info about Miro in Learning Centre here)
