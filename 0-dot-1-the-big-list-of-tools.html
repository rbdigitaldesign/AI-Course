Below is a list of AI tools that perform all manner of different tasks. This list is far from comprehensive and should not be taken as an endorsement by us to use any of these particular tools.

Generative text tools (general llms)

  • Copilot (Conversational AI assistant and productivity support)
  • ChatGPT (General purpose conversational AI and content generation)
  • Claude (Large language model focused on reasoning and long documents)
  • Gemini (Multimodal AI assistant and research support)
  • Perplexity AI (AI search engine with cited answers)

AI in-built browsers

  • Comet (AI-assisted web browsing and research environment)
  • Atlas (AI browsing and research workspace inside ChatGPT)

Local AI on your computer

  • LM Studio (Run large language models locally on your device)

Generative image tools

  • DALL·E (Text-to-image generation)
  • Midjourney (High-fidelity artistic image generation)
  • Stable Diffusion (Open-source image generation)
  • Firefly (Commercial-safe generative image creation)
  • Canva AI image generator (AI-assisted image and design creation)

Generative video tools

  • Runway Gen-3 (Text-to-video generation)
  • Pika (Prompt-based video creation)
  • Synthesia (AI avatar video generation)
  • Dream machine (AI video generation from prompts)
  • Descript (AI-assisted editing and overdubbing)
  • HeyGen (AI talking avatars and video presentation generation)
  • LipsyncAI (Automated lip-synchronisation for generated video)

Text-to-speech summary tools

  • NotebookLM (AI research assistant and audio summaries)

Translation tools

  • Google translate (Machine translation between languages)

Audio, music, & voice generation

  • ElevenLabs (Voice cloning and text-to-speech)
  • Whisper (Speech-to-text transcription)
  • AIVA (AI music composition)
  • Suno (Text-to-song generation)

Coding & development assistants

  • GitHub copilot (AI code completion and generation)
  • Ghostwriter (In-editor AI coding assistant)
  • CodeWhisperer (AI coding assistant for AWS workflows)
  • Tabnine (Predictive code completions)
  • Cursor (AI-native development environment)

Productivity & knowledge management

  • Notion AI (Integrated workspace AI)
  • Coda AI (Document automation and workflow logic)
  • Slack AI (AI workplace assistant and summaries)
  • Zapier AI (Automation and workflow orchestration)

Presentation & slide generation

  • Beautiful.ai (AI slide design)
  • Gamma (Documents to presentations automation)

3D & design

  • Spline AI (Text-to-3D scene generation)

Research mapping & evidence synthesis

  • ResearchRabbit (Visualising literature and citation networks)
  • Consensus (Evidence-based answers from research)
  • Elicit (Literature review automation)
  • Scite (Citation context and verification)
  • Connected papers (Graph-based paper exploration)
  • Semantic scholar (AI-enhanced literature search)

Academic writing & review

  • Paperpal (Academic writing enhancement)
  • Thesify (Editing and structured feedback)
  • GrammarlyGO (AI writing assistant)
  • QuillBot (Paraphrasing and summarisation)
  • Writefull (AI support for research writing)
