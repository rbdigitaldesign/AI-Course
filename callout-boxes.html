Teaching and Learning Callouts

 

Read

This box is used to direct a student to take an action - read this resource, in this case

Watch

  ~5 minutes

This box is used to direct a student to take an action - watch the following video (embed below this callout), or watch this resource (link inside of this callout).

Use the info-tag span to give an approximate run-time. This could also be used for reading activities of the approximate expected reading time.

Do

This box is used to direct a student to take an action - Do something. This is generic and can be changed up - perhaps to indicate an interactive element like an H5P, custom interactive or linking out to an external tool.

Discuss

Use this box to either begin a discussion inside of a Discussion board, or to link/prompt students to discuss something inside of a workshop/session or to signpost that the next page is a forum.

Content Warning

  Mentions of deceased   Depictions of abuse

Use the info tags for quick-reference of triggering content and this content for context and descriptions to help students make their own decisions about how to best engage with content while not missing any learning.
